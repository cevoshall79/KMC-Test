Information for Participants

Ready to show off your pony? Here's what you need to know about registering and staging.

  • Pre-Registration Fee: $25 (Guarantees a prime spot)
  • Day-of Registration: $30 (Space permitting)
  • Check-in: Entrants must check in between 7:00 AM and 8:30 AM. All cars must be parked by 8:45 AM.
  • Eligibility: Only Ford Mustangs (all years, models, and derivatives) are eligible to register.
  • No Day-of Sales: Selling cars or soliciting non-KMC-related business is prohibited.
