KMC ANNUAL SCHOLARSHIP

We believe in fostering the next generation of technicians and enthusiasts. The Kitsap Mustang Club awards an annual scholarship to a deserving local student.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a graduating senior from a local High School (Central Kitsap, North Kitsap, South Kitsap, Bainbridge Island, Bremerton, Chimacum, Christian, North Mason, Peninsula, Port Townsend, & Shelton).
  • Must be pursuing post-secondary education (vocation/technical school education in the automotive trades preferred).
  • Preference is given to students demonstrating financial need and community involvement.
  • Must submit a completed application and required essay by the deadline.
