KMC ANNUAL SCHOLARSHIP

We believe in fostering the next generation of technicians and enthusiasts. The Kitsap Mustang Club awards an annual scholarship to a deserving local student.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a graduating senior from a local High School (Bainbridge Island, Bremerton, Central Kitsap, North Kitsap, South Kitsap, Christian, North Mason, and Peninsula).
  • Must be pursuing post-secondary education (vocation/technical school education in the automotive trades).
  • Must submit a completed application by the deadline.
DOWNLOAD SCHOLARSHIP FLYER (PDF)

Application Details

Scholarship Value: $1,000

Application Window: Opens: February 15th | Deadline: April 15th

  • Download the official application form below.
  • All required materials must be postmarked by the deadline.
  • The scholarship can be used for enrollment at any accredited college, university or vocational technical school.
  • The recipient must enroll in the institution during the school year following graduation and be enrolled full time.
  • The scholarship money will be paid to the educational institution.
