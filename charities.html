DRIVEN TO GIVE BACK

The success of our events, especially Mustangs on the Waterfront, allows us to fund meaningful contributions to organizations serving Kitsap County.

South Kitsap Helpline

Our mission at South Kitsap Helpline is to provide access to healthy food and emergency resources to help meet the needs of people in crisis in our community.

Learn More →

Bremerton Foodline

The mission of the Bremerton Foodline is to provide food assistance and other services to reduce hunger and improve self-sufficiency for those within our community.

Learn More →

Fishline

Fishline envisions a vibrant Kitsap County where all neighbors have access to essential resources and the opportunity to thrive.

Learn More →

SUPPORT OUR MISSION

Proceeds raised at our events go back into the Kitsap community. We also accept cash donations, toiletries, and new socks for the charities we support.
