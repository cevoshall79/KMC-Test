OUR CLUB: DRIVEN BY HISTORY

The Kitsap Mustang Club (KMC) has been uniting Mustang enthusiasts in the beautiful Pacific Northwest since 1988.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to the preservation, enjoyment, and promotion of the Ford Mustang, from the 1964 1/2 classics to the latest models off the production line. Our mission is simple: to provide a welcoming, friendly, and family-oriented environment where people who share a passion for the pony car can connect, share knowledge, and give back to our local community.

We host monthly meetings, participate in local parades, organize scenic cruises across the Olympic Peninsula, and manage our signature annual event, Mustangs on the Waterfront.

Founding & Spirit

The KMC was founded in Port Orchard, Washington, by a small group of local owners who wanted to turn their shared hobby into a formal club. Over the decades, we've grown into a strong organization rooted in camaraderie and community service.

We are proud to be based in Kitsap County and actively support local charities and provide scholarships through the success of our events. We believe the Mustang spirit isn't just about horsepower‚Äîit's about pride, heritage, and fellowship.
